Dr. B. B. Gupta received PhD degree from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, India (in joint collaboration with University of Saskatchewan, Canada) in the area of Information and Cyber Security. He has excellent academic record throughout his career and was among the university toppers and got PhD admission directly after bachelorâ€™s degree. In 2009, he was selected for Canadian Commonwealth Scholarship award by Government of Canada.
